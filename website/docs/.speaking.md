### Interactive Illumination: Letting the Public Control My Christmas Lights

Take a peek behind the curtain of a high-tech holiday display that trades traditional light strings
for lights and code. This talk covers the journey of building a community-focused light show that anyone
with a smartphone can influence. From the basics of ESP32 controllers to the logic of open-source automation,
you’ll learn how to transform a hobby into a neighborhood landmark while keeping the system scalable and fun.
