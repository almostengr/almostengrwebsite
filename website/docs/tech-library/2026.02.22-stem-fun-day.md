Yesterday, members of NSBE Montgomery Professionals chapter, and other volunteers participated in STEM Fun Day. This 
event was free to high school students. Upward Bound program and Trenholm State were the leads for this program. 

The purpose of this event was to showcase the field of STEM to students who may not otherwise see it. Various project
kits were provide to the studetns, including music boxes, LEGO, and electrical projects.
